Welcome to Cyberpunk Edgerunners Quotes!

This page displays quotes and images from Cyberpunk Edgerunners.

Edgerunners is an animated series set in the dystopian and futuristic Night City, where modifying one's body into a weapon is glorified. Striving to "make it big", a boy named David joins a mercenary gang and fights his way to legendary status.

Initially serving as promotional material for the videogame Cyberpunk 2077, Cyberpunk Edgerunners quickly rose in popularity, earning a large audience even among non-gamers due to its emotional appeal and deep messages.
